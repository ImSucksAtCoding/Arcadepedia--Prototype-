Rules

1. Be nice and treat people with respect.

Keep discussions civil and be open-minded about differing opinions.

2. Do not ask for or share links to pirated material.

3. Spamming, trolling, or impersonating other users will lead to your account being banned.

4. Ensure that all posts, comments, and discussions are appropriate for all ages.

No obscene, offensive, or adult content, and keep discussions relevant to arcade video games and related topics.

5. Respect intellectual property rights.

Do not post content that you do not own or have permission to share.

6. Users are generally discouraged from creating multiple accounts.

If there is a legitimate reason for having more than one account, please inform the moderators.

7. If you see content that violates these rules, please bring it to the attention of the moderators through email feedback.
